About the movie

Eight years after Rakesh Mahadkar reigned terror on Mumbai, another serial killer has taken birth. More brutal and dangerous but he uses the same cover, the Smiley Mask. Ek Villain Returns is the story of two men in one sided love. The paths they choose to fulfill their love stories decide who the Hero is and who is the Villain.
